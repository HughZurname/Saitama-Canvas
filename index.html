The many faces of Saitama

Firstly, if you get the reference, well done!

Saitama is an expressive guy, for all of his lack of hair he can perform a surprising range of facial acrobatics.

You should know that he is also quite sensitive to interaction, if you touch his face in the following ways, you may discover some interesting reactions:

  • Poke him once in the right eye to make it water.
  • Poke his eye again and he may get upset.
  • Stroke his chin and watch a beard suddenly appear, when you're near.
  • Stroke his beard to watch it miraculously grow in length and majesty.
  • For some extra entertainment, click his forehead (fivehead?) for some excellent shades to help him deal with "it".

A final note, combine any of the effects and see what you get. Also Refreshing the page will give you a fab new background.
